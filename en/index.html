HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

From non-invasive inspection technology to evidence-based technical reports, we help you understand a property's true condition.

01 / Non-Invasive Structural Assessment

Using advanced inspection technology—including ground-penetrating radar, thermal imaging cameras, and drones—to detect hidden defects in plumbing, electrical systems, and structural elements without causing damage to the property. An ideal solution for property inspections and technical assessments.

02 / Evidence-Based Technical Assessments

Preparation of technical inspection reports designed to objectively and transparently reflect the property's construction condition, providing clear findings supported by measurable evidence. A professionally documented report that can support price negotiations, repair requests before closing, or help avoid costly real estate investments.

03 / Privacy & Asset Protection

Property inspections are carried out under strict protocols to preserve the property's original condition. We also guarantee complete confidentiality and data security: all technical reports and inspection records are encrypted, private, and accessible only to the client.
