Pre-Purchase & Pre-Rental Property Inspection

Comprehensive technical assessment of the property before signing a purchase or rental agreement, helping you make an informed decision with confidence.

Our inspection includes a thorough evaluation of:

  • Plumbing systems
  • Electrical systems
  • Gas installations
  • Structural condition
  • Moisture and water damage
  • Doors and windows
  • Construction materials and finish quality

Construction & Renovation Quality Inspection

Detailed technical inspection of newly built and recently renovated properties, ensuring the builder has met the agreed specifications and quality standards before handover.

  • Finishes and surface materials (engineered wood flooring, ceramic tiles, etc.)
  • Levelness and alignment
  • Proper operation of all building systems and installations

Property Defect Assessment

Specialized technical assessment for properties showing existing defects or signs of damage:

  • Water leaks and moisture intrusion
  • Structural cracks

to determine the root cause of the issue and recommend the most appropriate technical solution.
